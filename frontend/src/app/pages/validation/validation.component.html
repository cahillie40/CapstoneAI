What this model does

This prediction model estimates a player's likely performance score using both traditional football statistics and advanced analytics.

It combines attacking output, creativity, defensive contribution, discipline, injury status, and workload into a single predicted form rating between 0 and 100.

What improves a score

  • Goals and assists
  • Expected Goals (xG) and Expected Assists (xA)
  • Key passes and progressive passes
  • Dribbles completed
  • Tackles, interceptions, and ball recoveries
  • Strong pass accuracy

What reduces a score

  • Injury status
  • Red cards and yellow cards
  • Too many missed matches
  • Heavy recent match load
  • Lower overall attacking or defensive contribution

How scoring works

The model starts from a baseline score and then adds or subtracts value depending on the player's statistics.

Positive contributions increase the predicted form rating, while risk and availability factors reduce it.

Feature Weights

Larger positive weights help increase the score more strongly. Negative weights reduce the score.
